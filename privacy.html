1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how 4Connect (operated by Studio Kah) handles information when you use this website.

Studio Kah does not store or save user-created content through 4Connect, and we do not maintain personal user accounts on this site.

If you contact us directly by email, we may receive the information you include in your message so we can respond.

3. How information is used

Any information sent directly to us is used only to respond to inquiries, support requests, or feedback.

Studio Kah does not sell your personal information and does not share your information with third parties for independent marketing purposes.

We use reasonable measures to protect information we receive, but no transmission or storage method is completely secure.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@fourconnect.net or visit our Contact page.
