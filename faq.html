FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is 4Connect?

4Connect is a daily word puzzle where you sort 16 words into 4 correct groups of 4. Every group is connected by a shared idea, such as a category, phrase pattern, trivia theme, or similar relationship.

How often is there a new puzzle?

A new puzzle is published every day. If you already completed today's game, you can play earlier puzzles from the archive.

How many mistakes do I get?

You can make up to 4 incorrect guesses. If you miss 4 times, that round ends, and you can start over and try again.

How do I submit a group?

Select 4 words. Your guess is checked automatically once 4 words are selected. If the connection is correct, that set is locked and moved out of the active board.

Why do some words seem to fit more than one group?

That is intentional. Many puzzles include overlap or decoy matches to increase the challenge. The correct solution always has one valid arrangement of 4 groups of 4.

Are there hints?

4Connect currently does not include in-game hints. If you want strategy help, visit How to Play for rules and solving tips.

Does 4Connect track my stats or streaks?

A full stats system is not available yet. The Stats page is currently a placeholder and will be expanded in a future update.

Do I need an account to play?

No account is required. You can play directly in your browser with no sign-up.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes. 4Connect is designed to work on modern mobile and desktop browsers.

What data does 4Connect collect?

4Connect does not require user accounts and does not store user-created content. Third-party services such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense may collect usage data. For full details, see the Privacy Policy.

How do I report a bug or suggest a category?

Send feedback through the Contact page. We review bug reports, puzzle feedback, and category suggestions there.
