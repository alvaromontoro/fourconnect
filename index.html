How to Play

4Connect's goal is simple: find the hidden groups of four. Select four items and your guess will be submitted. If the group is correct, it's locked in and removed from the board. Keep grouping until all sets are found.

But choose carefully! The game only allows four mistakes. Can you find all four groups of four words with four mistakes or fewer?

Good luck!
