How to Play

4 words. 4 groups. 4 connections.

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Look at all 16 words* and scan for connections between them.
  2. Select 4 words that you think belong to the same category by tappingclicking on them.
  3. Once you have selected 4 words, they will be evaluated automatically.
  4. If the group is correct, it is locked and moved to the board's bottom.
  5. If the group is incorrect, the words will be deselected and you can try again.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining words until all 4 categories are found.
  7. If you fail 4 times, you'll lose... or you can start again. I'm not your boss.
*A word can be a any word, or a name, or even a number or a shape. It will depend on the puzzle.

Rules

  • Each puzzle has exactly 4 valid groups.
  • Each group contains exactly 4 words.
  • A word can belong to only one group.
  • Some words may look like they fit multiple categories, so choose carefully.

Tips

  • Start with the most obvious set first to reduce the board quickly.
  • Say words out loud to catch sound-based themes or common phrases.
  • If you are stuck, reshuffle your thinking and test a different connection.
